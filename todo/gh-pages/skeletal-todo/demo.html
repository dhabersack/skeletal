Skeletal element demo

A solid foundation for clean webdesign.

Skeletal is a preset-stylesheet that sets sensible defaults for most HTML5-tags, providing a solid foundation to build your next webdesign project on.

What it is

Clean
Skeletal puts an emphasis on readability and clean organization to make the sites using it as simple and user-friendly as possible.
Easily configurable
Everything you might want to change can easily be adapted through straightforward configuration files. This includes fonts, colors, grids and more.
Responsive
Our SASS-powered grid grows with the browser. Responsiveness is baked right in and available through the grid-mixin.
Not Bootstrap
Other than options like Bootstrap, your markup won't need any additional attributes and stays maintainable and flexible.
